With the help of image recognition algorithms, the eye-tracker can estimate gaze points very accurately by knowing the position of the head and eyes, the distance to the screen a participant is looking at and the eye-tracker's position. Eye-tracking is a helpful technology for many applications. For example, it can help detect tiredness while driving or it can support applications for screening and training purposes in the medical domain. Eye-tracking is also used in gaming, marketing, and human-computer interaction.
